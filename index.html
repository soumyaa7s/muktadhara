Our Story

Founded in 2020, Muktadhara began as a small family business with a simple mission: to bring authentic Indian flavors to every household.

What started as homemade recipes passed down through generations has now become India's most loved snack brand. We believe in using only the finest ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and innovative packaging to deliver freshness and quality.
