About Us

Muktadhara is a brand that brings the authentic flavors of India to your table. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide people with the pure taste of India through our handcrafted snacks and biscuits.

Read our story

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate food enthusiasts, Muktadhara was born from a desire to share the rich culinary heritage of India with the world. Each product is crafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring that every bite delivers the authentic taste you crave.

Growth & Expansion

Over the years, Muktadhara has grown from a small local brand to a trusted name in the Indian snack industry. We have expanded our product range and distribution network to reach more customers across the country, while maintaining our commitment to quality and authenticity.
